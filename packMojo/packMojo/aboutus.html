About PackMojo

Our Mission

At our core, we believe that packaging is more than just a box. It's a powerful tool that businesses can use to tell their brand story and create impactful first impressions with customers. Our mission is to empower businesses of all sizes to unlock the benefits of custom packaging so that every interaction with your brand is a meaningful one.

Our Solutions

We know that getting custom packaging can often be confusing and overwhelming. Through our platform, we streamline the packaging workflow to make the process of buying custom packaging easy and transparent, with dedicated support every step of the way. Whether you're a startup or global enterprise, we’ve got you covered.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We believe that sustainable packaging should be the default choice. That’s why we partner with factories that produce paper and cardboard based packaging, ranging from custom boxes to custom printed labels, that are sustainable by default.

The core team

Wendy Chan, CEO

Wendy is the co-founder and CEO of PackMojo. She is an expert in B2B customer acquisition, marketing, and product development. Prior to PackMojo, Wendy was the COO of PassKit, a SaaS company that provides mobile wallet and loyalty solutions. In 2018, Wendy was listed on Forbes 30 under 30 Asia, and during her free time, she enjoys exercising, learning Spanish, and listening to podcasts.

Vincent Choi, COO

Vincent is the co-founder and COO of PackMojo. He is an expert in printing and packaging, and is currently a co-owner of a packaging factory based in China. With deep knowledge in the industry, Vincent is passionate about modernizing the space and creating an accessible platform that fulfills today’s packaging needs. In his free time, he enjoys spending time in the gym, playing basketball and is an avid Houston Rockets fan.

Christian Heine, CTO

Christian joined the PackMojo team in early 2021. He has extensive experience in information technology, software development, and product management, having worked in a variety of countries and roles. In the most recent years before PackMojo, he served as VP of IT for a stock-listed multinational company and had launched a technology-focused startup. He is passionate about food, non-fiction books, podcasts, and exercise.
